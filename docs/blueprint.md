# **App Name**: YieldIQ

## Core Features:

- Farm Profile & Data Input: Allows farmers to securely input and manage their farm's essential data, such as crop types, soil conditions, planting dates, and location, serving as the foundation for AI analysis.
- AI-Powered Yield Prediction & Optimization Tool: Utilizes Google Vertex AI to train machine learning models that predict crop yields and provide suggestions for optimizing water, fertilizer, and pest management based on historical and real-time data.
- Personalized Advisory Report Generation: Generates clear, easy-to-understand advisory reports from AI predictions, offering actionable guidance for farming practices, planning storage, sales, and estimating profits.
- Local Language Conversational Assistant: Integrates Google Gemini 1.5 Pro to provide a natural language understanding tool for farmers to ask questions and receive advice in local languages like Hindi and Marathi.
- Real-time Alerts & Notifications: Sends timely notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging for critical updates such as weather changes, potential crop diseases, and irrigation schedules, keeping farmers informed.
- GIS-Powered Farm & Field Visualization: Leverages Google Earth Engine and Google Maps APIs to provide interactive field maps and satellite analysis, offering visual insights into farm health and land conditions.
- Secure Data Storage with Firestore: Ensures all user, farm, and operational data is securely stored and managed within Firebase Firestore, as per user's specification.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant, nature-inspired green (#51A329) for professionalism and growth, balancing the intelligence of the platform with its agricultural purpose.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated green (#F1F5F0) that provides a clean, open canvas, maintaining harmony with the primary color while ensuring readability.
- Accent color: A lively yellow-green (#63EB47) that stands out to highlight key information, calls to action, and indicators of health or success.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, objective readability across all text sizes, ensuring that complex data and advisory reports are easily consumed by farmers.
- Clean, modern line icons with subtle references to agricultural elements (e.g., leaves, water drops, sun) to intuitively represent features and data points. Avoid overly cartoonish or highly detailed styles.
- A highly responsive, card-based dashboard layout designed to prioritize clarity and accessibility of critical information, with mobile-first optimization for smartphone users.
- Subtle and functional animations for transitions, loading states, and data visualizations. Animations should be swift and not detract from the information, providing positive feedback to user interactions.
